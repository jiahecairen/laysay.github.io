Mug Of Completist
I have a special mug,this mug is produce by Nintendo corporation,a video-games company.
Why is the mug special?
There are six black heart-shaped pattern on the mug wall,there are three on each side,you can know it if you’ve played a video game “The Legend of Zelda: The Breath of The Wild”,when you pour hot water into the mug,the color of these heart-shaped pattern will change from black to red.
By the way,the mug in my hand is my fourth mug,I spent about two hundred to get it.
My first mug was sold by me,because that mug had some defect,for example there are some black spots on the mug wall,and then,when I use the mug to drink water I always to care about spot,about one month later,being a completist,I decided sold it.
Much later,after a half year, I sudden want to get other one,I can’t explain the desire,maybe it’s just because I like it,it’s as simple as that.Unfortunately,when I receive my second mug,it had a bigger defect.My first mug’s heart-shaped pattern is red,but the second mug’s heart-spaped pattern is light red,almost like pink!
I can’t bear it,so I got other new one,but when I receive the third mug,I felt hopeless…
I couldn’t believe what I saw,the third mug also had the seme wrong,So I thought about it for a moment,I think the second one and third one was produced this year,but the first is production last year,therefore their colors were different,I can get a great mug that I want if I buy a mug that was produced last year.
I finally bought the fourth mug in second-hand market,to be honest,the fourth mug isn’t very perfect,but I don’t want to spend more time and energy to get better other one,I can remember the target that to get a perfect mug,wait until I achieve my main goal before it’s too late to buy the mug of my dreams.
As a completist,it’s too difficult to accept something that has defects,but I always tell myself,”Life needs to done,not perfect.”
Though I want to do everything perfect,but I have to finish more important things,those umimportent things can be finish in the future,and I thought also,maybe in the future,I will forget it.

Chinese version 「完美主义者的杯子」
我有一个特别的杯子，这个杯子是由任天堂公司（Nintendo Corporation）生产的，是一家电子游戏公司。
为什么这个杯子特别呢？
杯子的壁上有六个黑色的心形图案，每边有三个。如果你玩过一款名为《塞尔达传说：荒野之息》的游戏，你就会知道它。当你将热水倒入杯子时，这些心形图案的颜色会从黑色变为红色。
顺便说一下，我手上的这个杯子是我第四个杯子，我花了大约两百元买到它。
我的第一个杯子我卖掉了，因为它有一些瑕疵，比如杯壁上有一些黑点。然后，当我用这个杯子喝水时，我总是担心那些斑点。大约一个月后，作为一个完美主义者，我决定把它卖掉。
过了很久，大约半年后，我突然又想要另一个。我无法解释这种欲望，也许只是因为我喜欢它，就是这么简单。不幸的是，当我收到第二个杯子时，它有一个更大的瑕疵。我的第一个杯子的心形图案是红色的，但第二个杯子的心形图案是浅红色的，几乎像粉红色！
我无法忍受，所以我又买了一个新的。但是当我收到第三个杯子时，我感到绝望...
我无法相信我所看到的，第三个杯子也有同样的问题。所以我思考了一会儿，我觉得第二个和第三个是今年生产的，而第一个是去年生产的，因此它们的颜色不同。如果我买一个去年生产的杯子，我就可以得到我想要的一个很棒的杯子。
最后，我在二手市场上买到了第四个杯子，说实话，第四个杯子并不完美，但我不想再花更多的时间和精力去找更好的杯子。我记得自己的目标是得到一个完美的杯子，在实现主要目标之前，等待时机再买我梦寐以求的杯子。
作为一个完美主义者，接受有瑕疵的东西太困难了，但我总是告诉自己：“生活需要去做，而不是追求完美。”
尽管我想把每件事都做得完美，但我必须完成更重要的事情，那些不重要的事情可以在将来完成。我也觉得，也许在将来，我会忘记这件事。
